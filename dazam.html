Dazam

What problem does Dazam solve?

A couple of months ago I started to take ballroom dancing lessons. With enough practice I managed to memorise the steps for the different dance styles reasonable well but I kept on struggling to figure out to what song I can dance what dance style. So I decided to develop a little app which can help me out. This is how Dazam was born. Itâ€™s a ML based dance style recognition app.

How does the Dazam work?

With Dazam you can record a 15 second audio snippet and you get back predictions for the top five dance styles which can be danced to the audio snippet. I have tried to different ML models for the task. The first model is a Convolutional Neural Network which takes an image of the spectrogram as input and has a simple softmax output.
